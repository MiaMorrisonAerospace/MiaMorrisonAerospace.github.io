About Me

Hi, I'm Mia Morrison.

I'm an Aerospace Engineering student at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University who enjoys solving complex problems through systems thinking, leadership, and engineering design.

Long before studying engineering, I was already focused on solving challenges in my community. As a two-time Florida Youth of the Year, I developed a college readiness program that helped students navigate applications, scholarships, essays, and the transition to higher education. Through advocacy, public speaking, and community leadership, I learned how to identify problems, communicate solutions, and bring people together around a common goal.

Today, I apply that same mindset to engineering. Whether designing autonomous rover systems, developing software tools, or creating mechanical designs, I enjoy understanding how individual components interact within a larger system and developing practical solutions that create real impact.

My goal is to help design and develop aerospace systems that push the boundaries of exploration, flight, and technology while leading teams that turn ambitious ideas into reality.
